About the Platform

LegallySafe implements a RAG-Enabled Transformer Architecture specifically tuned for multilingual legal document understanding. By combining large language models with a proprietary vector database of legal statutes, the system provides high-accuracy compliance checking that minimizes "hallucinations" common in standard AI models.
